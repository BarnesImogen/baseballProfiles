# How to Add Your Baseball Photos

To incorporate your baseball photos into the website, follow these steps:

## 1. Save Your Photos

Save your photos to the `/public/images/` folder with these exact names:

- `catcher-1.jpg` - First catching photo
- `catcher-2.jpg` - Second catching photo  
- `catcher-3.jpg` - Third catching photo (also used as hero background)
- `teamwork.jpg` - Photo with teammate
- `batting-1.jpg` - First batting photo
- `batting-2.jpg` - Second batting photo (also used in contact section background)
- `action.jpg` - Game action photo

## 2. Location

Place all images in:
```
/Users/imogen/barnsey-baseball/public/images/
```

## 3. Supported Formats

- `.jpg` (recommended)
- `.jpeg`
- `.png`
- `.webp`

## 4. Recommended Image Sizes

- Gallery photos: 800x600px or larger
- Hero background: 1920x1080px or larger

## 5. The photos will automatically appear in:

- **Hero Section Background**: Uses `catcher-3.jpg`
- **Photo Gallery**: Displays all 6 photos in a responsive grid
- **Contact Section Background**: Uses `batting-2.jpg` as a subtle background

Simply drag and drop your photos into the `/public/images/` folder and refresh the browser!
